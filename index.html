Importance of all instruments in a song

Drums
These are like the backbone of most songs to keep all other performers/intruments in time.

Bass
A bass or bass line is vital for any song. This provides the low notes that you will harmonize with the melody to take a song the the next level

Melody
The melody is what everyone will hear right away so it must be one of the more important parts of the song whether that be singing or a non-lyrical song
